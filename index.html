Common questions

Straight answers. No runaround.

How much does it cost?
It depends on what you need. A single landing page starts at $500. A full custom site with SEO, integrations, and copywriting runs $1,200 to $3,500. Marketing retainers start at $400 per month. AI builds are scoped per project. Every engagement starts with a free call where I tell you exactly what it would cost before you commit to anything.
How long does it take to build a site?
Most sites are live within 5 to 10 business days. I show you a working draft before anything goes live so you can review and request changes. Complex builds with custom functionality take 2 to 3 weeks. You will never be left waiting without an update.
How do I know this will actually work?
Look at the reviews above. Cody Sargent had a site live and generating customer inquiries within days. Rafael Diaz at Esthers Sons Kitchen saw his online presence transform. Zackary Hanna at Licom AI had a polished site that matched the quality of his product. I do not take on projects I cannot deliver on — and I will tell you that upfront on the call if we're not the right fit.
Do I have to sign a long contract?
No. One-time projects are a simple agreement with a clear scope and deliverables. Monthly retainers are month-to-month — you stay because you're seeing results, not because a contract forces you to. I keep it simple because I have to earn the business every month.
I already have a website. Can you improve it?
Yes — and that is often the fastest win. I will audit your current site for free, identify exactly what is holding it back (speed, structure, copy, SEO), and give you a clear plan. Sometimes it's a few targeted fixes. Sometimes a full rebuild makes more sense. Either way you'll know before spending anything.
